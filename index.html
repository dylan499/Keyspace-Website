The Process

How It Works

A complete, four-stage process that takes you from first conversation to a fully performing, cash-flow-positive property asset.

1

Land Sourcing

We identify and secure investment-grade land in high-demand Australian markets — targeting locations suited for high-yield strategies like rooming houses and cash-flow developments.

2

Finance Structuring

We work alongside your broker or introduce our trusted finance network to structure the right lending solution for your investment goals and serviceability position.

3

Development & Build

Our experienced development team manages design, approvals, and construction end-to-end — delivering a completed, tenancy-ready asset on time and on budget.

4

Property Management

Once built, we manage your asset for maximum yield and occupancy — regular reporting, real-time insights, and fully hands-off operations for you.

Common Questions

Questions investors ask us

Straight answers to the most common questions before a strategy session.

A rooming house is a residential property with multiple private bedrooms, each leased to individual tenants with a shared common area. Because you're collecting rent from 5–8 tenants instead of one household, total weekly income is significantly higher — typically generating gross yields of 10–14% compared to 3–5% on a standard rental.
This depends on your current borrowing capacity, existing equity, and the specific project. We structure each investment individually — some clients use equity from existing properties, others use cash deposits. We'll assess your full financial position during the strategy session before recommending anything.
Typically 12–18 months from land settlement to first tenant income. This covers council approvals, construction, and tenancy setup. The timeline varies by location and project complexity. We'll give you a project-specific schedule during the planning phase.
We have a trusted network of brokers experienced with development and rooming house finance structures. We can introduce you to the right specialist, or work alongside your existing broker if you have one. What matters is that the lending is structured correctly for the project — we'll make sure it is.
Yes — SMSF investment is a strategy several of our clients use. There are specific borrowing rules that apply (LRBA structures), and not every project is suited to SMSF. We work with SMSF-specialist advisers and can discuss whether this structure makes sense for your situation.
A buyers agent finds you a property. A property manager looks after it after you buy. Keyspace does both — and everything in between. We source the land, assist with finance, manage the build, and operate the asset. You're not co-ordinating five different parties; you have one strategic partner handling the entire lifecycle.
We primarily focus on high-growth corridors in Victoria and Queensland — markets where rooming house demand is strong, council approval pathways are established, and rental vacancy is low. We assess each location based on yield potential, infrastructure pipeline, and population growth data.
Our property management team takes over immediately — handling tenant sourcing, background checks, leasing, and ongoing maintenance.
